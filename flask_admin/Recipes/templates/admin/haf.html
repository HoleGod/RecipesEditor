Help & FAQ

To register, click the "Register" button in the top menu, fill out the form, and confirm your email address.

Go to the "Add Recipe" section, fill in the required fields, and click "Submit".

Log in to your profile, click "Edit Profile", and make the necessary changes.

Navigate to the recipe page, scroll to the comments section, write your comment in the input box, and click the "Submit" button. You must be logged in to add comments.

On the recipe page, click on "Add to Favorites" button to save the recipe to your favorites list. You must be logged in to use this feature.
